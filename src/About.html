In 1964, Blood Center was born in Addis Ababa. It was founded in response to the need for a reliable, local blood supply. Challenged with a vision to provide a safe and reliable community blood supply, our founders sought the cooperation and partnership of community and corporate leaders, employees, blood donors, volunteers, and contributors.

Our vission

  • Inspiring people to give blood
  • Increasing communication with our members.
  • Crearing responsible society in the world.

The Community Blood Center is about connecting lives and sharing life. As a local nonprofit organization, our patients and hospitals depend on us to provide a steady and reliable blood supply. And we rely on you—volunteer blood donors and blood drive sponsors—to provide the lifesaving blood and blood products needed.

Blood donation is that important. We need to ensure a steady blood supply will be available so we can respond to traumas and urgent needs as they arise.
